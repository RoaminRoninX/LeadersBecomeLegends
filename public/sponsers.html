CORDR

CORDR is an integrated waste & recycling organization that emphasizes its main focal point on, assisting businesses with reducing their carbon footprint.Through this focus, they are committed to providing reliable waste & recycling services, complimented by friendly customer service and sustainable best management practices.

IMAGINE ENERGY

Imagine Energy's mission is to harness the power of solar energy to elevate local communities in the Pacific Northwest by promoting environmental sustainability, workforce development, and resiliency. Meyer Memorial Trust

Meyer Memorial Trust was created by the late Fred G. Meyer, founder of a chain of retail stores bearing his name throughout the Pacific Northwest. Established in 1982, Meyer has awarded roughly $840 million in grants and program-related investments to more than 3,380 organizations in Oregon and Southwest Washington.

Learn More Here →

BUDGET INSURANCE ENTERPRISES

Budget Insurane Enterprises have years of insurance experience helping clients prepare for the unknown. They offer Home, Auto, Fire, Flood and Theft insurance. They offer a personalized business or individual assessment and find out how they can help protect your hard-won assets.

PROSPER PORTLAND

Prosper Portland is an economic and urban development agency for the city of Portland. They focus on building an equitable economy by carrying out a comprehensive range of economic development programs that support small business, improve access to workforce training, and create jobs for Portland residents.

Learn More Here →

OSEIA

OSEIA is a trade association that promotes clean, renewable, solar technologies. They provide focus on the solar value chain; from workforce development to permitting, advocacy, policy, and regulation for residential, commercial, community, and utility scale solar projects on the local, state and regional level.

Learn More Here →

ROSEWOOD INITIATIVE

The Rosewood initiative is to build a safe, healthy and vibrant community where neighbors can thrive together.Neighbors want to feel accepted, welcome and safe in their community. Neighbors want affordability, in housing and in life. Neighbors want to see positive, healthy businesses and amenities that are easily accessible.

LINE FOR LIFE

Lines for Life is a regional non-profit dedicated to preventing substance abuse and suicide. They offer help and hope to individuals and communities, and promote mental health for all. their work addresses a spectrum of needs that include intervention, prevention, and advocacy.

VERDE

Verde builds wealth through social enterprise, outreach and advovacy. Verde has brought environmental investments to Portland's neighborhoods, involved comunity members in the planning and building of these investments, and ensure that low-income people and people of color directly benefited from the investments.

The Portland Clean Energy Fund

PCEF means green jobs and healthy homes for all residents of Portland. The Portland Clean Energy Fund will distribute approximately $50 million each year to support projects like solar panels, living-wage job training, trees and urban gardens, and home upgrades including better insulation, heating and cooling, and energy efficiency.
